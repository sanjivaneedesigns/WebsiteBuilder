How It Works

An experience built for innovation

Unfold Ideas isn't just a tool — it's a structured experience that guides your organization from problem statement to actionable, validated solutions.

01
Step 01

Launch a Challenge

Define your innovation brief and invite participants from across your organization or ecosystem. Set timelines, themes, and success criteria with guided templates.

  • Customizable challenge templates
  • Multi-role participant management
  • Structured problem framing tools
02
Step 02

Collect Ideas

Participants submit, refine, and build on ideas in a structured workspace. AI-assisted clustering surfaces the most promising concepts from the crowd.

  • Guided idea submission forms
  • Real-time collaboration tools
  • AI-powered idea clustering

Step 03

Create Solutions

Expert panels evaluate top ideas and teams develop them into actionable plans. Track milestones and measure real-world impact from a central dashboard.

  • Structured evaluation rubrics

  • Impact tracking dashboard
