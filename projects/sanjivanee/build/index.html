How we work together

  1. 01

    Discovery Call (30 min)

    We talk about your challenge, your team, and your timeline. No sales pitch — just diagnosis. I'll tell you honestly whether a sprint is the right move.

  2. 02

    Sprint Design

    I map the sprint challenge, design the five-day agenda, and brief your team. You show up ready. I handle the preparation.

  3. 03

    Sprint Week

    Five days of focused work: understand, sketch, decide, prototype, test. Your team participates. Your stakeholders see the output. A real decision gets made.

  4. 04

    Follow-Through

    Post-sprint debrief, findings documentation, and a clear next-step recommendation. Some teams go straight into the next sprint. Some need space to absorb and execute. Both are fine.
