# Metaskills — Site Blueprint
**Template:** Authority (Dual-Audience Modified)
**Architecture:** Single Page, Anchor-Scroll Navigation
**Goal:** Dual-Track Lead Generation — Employer Inquiries + Candidate Registrations

---

## Strategy Lock

### Positioning Statement
> Metaskills is Bengaluru's boutique recruitment firm that gives both employers and talent an equal, premium experience — pre-vetted quality hiring for fast-growing companies, genuine advocacy for professionals ready for their next move.

### StoryBrand: Employers
- **Their frustration:** Agencies send CVs in bulk. Interview time gets wasted. Bad hires cost months.
- **Metaskills as Guide:** 10 years of pre-vetting. Boutique speed. Founder accountability.
- **The Plan:** Submit vacancy → Vetted shortlist in days → Make the right hire.
- **Success:** Right hire, faster, without the back-and-forth.
- **Failure (cost of inaction):** Another 3-month search. Another bad fit. Another costly exit.

### StoryBrand: Candidates
- **Their frustration:** Agencies ghost you, spam you with irrelevant roles, treat you as pipeline.
- **Metaskills as Guide:** We only present you when there's a genuine match. We advocate, not just transact.
- **The Plan:** Submit CV → Profile match → Relevant opportunities only.
- **Success:** A role that actually fits — company, culture, and career trajectory.
- **Failure (cost of inaction):** Keep being ignored or misrepresented by volume-first firms.

### Messaging Pillars
1. **Boutique Quality** — Pre-vetted candidates. Shorter cycles. No bulk-send CVs.
2. **Founder Accountability** — Led by Neelanjana Choudhury. A person, not a portal.
3. **Both Sides Matter** — Employers and candidates are both clients.
4. **Sector Depth** — IT, Leadership, Startups. Bengaluru ecosystem experts.
5. **Proven** — 10 years. SME Business of the Year 2023. Bosch, Keka, Incedo.
