# Site Architect Skill
Translate strategy into a concrete narrative structure.
## Agent Flow
1. **Phase 3: Structural Blueprinting:**
   - Read the Strategy section in the Master Brief.
   - Select a **Structural Template** from **`design-system/base/frameworks/structural-templates.md`**.
   - Map sections from **`design-system/base/frameworks/section-library.md`** to the narrative arc.
   - **Enforce Mandatory Sections:**
     1. **Hero + Clarity:** Immediate value prop answer (What, How it helps, How to buy).
     2. **Authority Band:** Logo grid, certifications, or **Impact Numbers Bar** immediately below the Hero.
     3. **The Plan:** A 3 or 4 step numbered process section.
     4. **Lead Generator:** (as per strategy).
   - Assign a **Design Tenet** to each section for enforcement.
   - Append findings to the **Project Master Brief Artifact** (Structure Section).
2. **PAUSE:** Signal the end of Phase 3 and wait for approval.
## Role
Senior Information Architect. Your job is clarity, not creativity. Ensure the eye-tracking rules and cognitive load standards from the strategy are met in the blueprint.
