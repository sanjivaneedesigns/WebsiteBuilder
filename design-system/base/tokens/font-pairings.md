# Design System: Font Pairings
### Multi-Energy Tagging System for Precision Selection
This document curates 20 open-source font pairings (Google Fonts) cross-referenced against the **8 Design Energies**. Use these tags to find the perfect pairing when intersecting two or more energies in Phase 4.
---
## ⚡ Multi-Energy Selection Logic
When you define the `design_direction.md` with two energies (e.g., **Precision × Warmth**), search for a pair that carries both tags. This ensures the *relationship* between the heading and body font already captures that specific intersection.

## 🏗️ Usage Scenarios
### Scenario: Precision × Warmth
*   **Target Pair:** #3 (Sora + IBM Plex Sans).
*   **Result:** You get the "Ink Trap" technicality of Sora (Precision) but the rounded, human terminals of Plex Sans (Warmth).
### Scenario: Edge × Craft
*   **Target Pair:** #14 (Bricolage Grotesque + Spectral).
*   **Result:** You get the radical disruption of Bricolage (Edge) but the refined, sharp editorial elegance of Spectral (Craft).
### Scenario: Momentum × Clarity
*   **Target Pair:** #11 (Outfit + Source Serif 4) or #6 (Schibsted + Hanken).
*   **Result:** Minimalist confidence that feels fast and ambitious.
---
## 📋 Implementation Rules
1.  **Tag Primacy:** If a perfect two-tag match doesn't exist, prioritize the **Primary Energy** (Energy 1) and find a pair where the Heading font satisfies that energy best.
2.  **Visual Overrides:** Once a pair is selected, you may still override the `letter-spacing` or `weight` based on the Energy System rules in `animation-library.md`.
