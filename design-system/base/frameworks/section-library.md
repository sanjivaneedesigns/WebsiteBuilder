# Section Library
Categories and types of sections available for the Site Architect.
## 1. Hero
*The first thing they see. Sets the tone.*
- **Statement Hero:** Large typography, centered, focus on value prop.
- **Visual Hero:** Image-heavy (left or right), clear sub-header.
- **Split Hero:** 50/50 split between copy and image/video.
- **Video Hero:** Immersive video background with minimal copy.
## 2. Social Proof
*Building trust via third parties.*
- **Logo Grid:** Black/white logos of clients or partners.
- **Testimonials:** Cards with headshots, names, and quotes.
- **Impact Numbers Bar:** Large numeric achievements (e.g., "500+ Clients," "10 Years Experience").
- **Press Bar:** Logos of publications.
## 3. Value & Features
*Explaining the 'What' and 'Why'.*
- **3-Column Features:** Classic grid with icons and short descriptions.
- **Side-by-Side:** Alternating image/copy blocks for deeper explanation.
- **Icon Grid:** High-density grid for minor features.
- **Accordion:** For complex features or "FAQs".
## 4. Process
*How the solution is delivered.*
- **Numbered Steps:** 1-2-3 horizontal or vertical flow.
- **Timeline:** Vertical line showing progress or history.
- **Waterfall:** Continuous scroll revealing steps.
## 5. Evidence
*Proof of the result.*
- **Case Study Snippet:** Summarized result + "Read More".
- **Before/After:** Slider or side-by-side comparison.
- **Results Metrics:** Large numbers with context (e.g., "300% Growth").
## 6. Call to Action (CTA)
*The final push.*
- **Full-width Banner:** High-contrast background with a single clear button.
- **Inline Form:** Simple email field for newsletter or waitlist.
- **Booking Embed:** Calendly or similar widget integrated.
## 7. Contact & Footer
- **Form Block:** Direct contact form.
- **Contact Card:** Phone, email, address + social links.
- **Map Embed:** Google Maps integration for local businesses.
