# Design Energy System
The visual framework for defining a unique aesthetic identity.
---
## 1. The 8 Energies
| Energy | Feeling | Visual Territory |
|---|---|---|
| **Precision** | Controlled, expert | Dense grids, monospace, cool neutrals |
| **Warmth** | Human, trusted | Organic shapes, serif, warm off-whites |
| **Momentum** | Fast, ambitious | Diagonal layouts, high contrast, kinetic type |
| **Depth** | Intellectual, layered | Heavy typographic hierarchy, muted palettes |
| **Clarity** | Simple, confident | Extreme whitespace, single accent |
| **Edge** | Disruptive, bold | Asymmetry, unexpected color, rule-breaking |
| **Craft** | Artisanal, human | Texture, imperfection, tactile warmth |
| **Trust** | Institutional, reliable | Traditional structure, conservative palette |
---
## 2. Intersections
Combine 2 energies to find a brand's unique "Visual Zone":
- `Precision × Warmth` → Boutique Consultancy
- `Momentum × Clarity` → Performance Fintech
- `Craft × Depth` → Considered Design Studio
- `Edge × Trust` → Grounded Challenger Brand
---
## 3. The Expressive Layer Rule
To maintain visual balance, only **ONE** layer can be "Expressive" (The Hero). All others must be "Restrained."
- **Layers:** Typography, Color, Motion, Spacing, or Imagery.
