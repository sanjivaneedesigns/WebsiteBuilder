# Structural Templates
Narrative blueprints designed for specific business goals.
## 1. The Authority
**Best For:** Professional services, consultancies, legal, finance.
**Objective:** Establish expertise and dissolve skepticism.
**Arc Logic:**
1. **Hero:** Clear value prop + authoritative sub-header.
2. **Social Proof:** Logos of known clients or "As seen in".
3. **Problem/Solution:** Aggravate the status quo, offer the proprietary approach.
4. **Value:** Core services with deep expertise framing.
5. **Evidence:** Detailed case study or high-impact metric.
6. **Detailed Proof:** Testimonials with names and titles.
7. **The Close:** Authoritative CTA (e.g., "Request Consultation").
## 2. The Product
**Best For:** SaaS, apps, physical tools.
**Objective:** Showcase utility, ease of use, and efficiency.
**Arc Logic:**
1. **Hero:** Visual of the product + immediate utility statement.
2. **Social Proof:** User count or "Built by" credentials.
3. **The "How it Works":** 3–4 steps to value.
4. **Feature Deep-Dive:** Interactive or visual feature breakdown.
5. **Comparison:** Why this product wins (vs. manual work or competitors).
6. **Social Proof:** User testimonials focusing on ROI/speed.
7. **Action:** Direct signup or trial CTA.
## 3. The Story
**Best For:** Creative agencies, studios, personal brands.
**Objective:** Build emotional connection and showcase personality.
**Arc Logic:**
1. **Hero:** Narrative-led hook + bold visual.
2. **The Ethos:** Why we do what we do (philosophical alignment).
3. **The Work:** Curated, large-scale portfolio showcase.
4. **The Process:** How the magic happens (behind the scenes).
5. **Human Proof:** Quotes about the relationship, not just the result.
6. **Soft CTA:** "Let's talk" or "View the lab".
## 4. The Local
**Best For:** Physical shops, hospitality, local services.
**Objective:** Drive physical traffic or local booking.
**Arc Logic:**
1. **Hero:** Location-specific value prop + high-quality local imagery.
2. **Immediate Info:** "Open Now", "Located at [Area]".
3. **The Offering:** Service menu or product categories.
4. **Local Proof:** Testimonials from community members.
5. **Logistics:** Map, Hours, Booking form.
6. **Secondary CTA:** Instagram feed or local newsletter.
## 5. The Launch
**Best For:** Pre-seed startups, waitlists, new book/product drops.
**Objective:** Build hype and capture emails/interest.
**Arc Logic:**
1. **Hero:** The "Big Idea" statement + scarcity hook.
2. **The Struggle:** Why the world needs this *now*.
3. **The Reveal:** What's coming (feature highlights).
4. **The Benefit:** What your life looks like post-launch.
5. **CTA:** Massive email field / "Join the Waitlist".
6. **Validation:** "Joined by [X] others" or "Backed by [Y]".
