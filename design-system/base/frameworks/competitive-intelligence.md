# Competitive Intelligence Framework
Guidelines for extracting strategic signals and identifying market gaps.
---
## 1. Identification (5-Type Framework)
When sourcing competitors, aim for this mix:
1. **Direct:** Same solution, same audience.
2. **Indirect:** Different solution, same problem.
3. **Aspirational:** Category leaders (who we want to be like).
4. **Adjacent:** Different industry, similar vibe/behavior.
5. **Niche/Local:** Small players doing one thing exceptionally well.
---
## 2. Extraction Guide (Signals)
For each site, extract the following behavioral signals:
- **Hero Signal:** Copy hierarchy + value prop clarity.
- **CTA Signal:** Text, placement, and friction level.
- **Narrative Signal:** The sequence of sections (Section Library types).
- **Proof Signal:** How they establish trust (logos, metrics, faces).
- **Energy Signal:** Visual tone (mapped to the 8 Energies).
---
## 3. Synthesis Rules (The Forced Decision)
Move from observations to a mandate for differentiation:
- **Market Patterns:** The "sea of sameness" (what everyone is doing).
- **Strategic Gaps:** Weaknesses or predictable patterns in competitors.
- **The Forced Decision:** A definitive statement of what we WILL NOT do and what we WILL do to stand out.
