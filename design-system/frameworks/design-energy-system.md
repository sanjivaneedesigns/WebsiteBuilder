# Design Energy System Framework

This framework categorizes and defines the distinct visual energies that can be applied to website designs.

## Overview

Design energy describes the emotional and aesthetic character of a design. Use this framework to select and apply the right energy for each project.

## Energy Categories

- **Bold & Commanding** – High contrast, strong typography, assertive layouts
- **Minimal & Clean** – White space, restrained palette, elegant simplicity
- **Playful & Dynamic** – Vibrant colors, organic shapes, expressive motion
- **Luxurious & Premium** – Refined details, rich textures, sophisticated palette
- **Tech & Innovative** – Geometric precision, dark themes, data-driven aesthetics
