# Structural Templates Framework

This framework provides pre-defined page structures and layout templates for common website types.

## Overview

Structural templates define the overall page composition — the order, proportion, and relationship of sections for different page goals.

## Template Types

- **SaaS Landing Page** – Hero > Features > Social Proof > Pricing > CTA
- **Agency Portfolio** – Hero > Work > Services > About > Contact
- **E-commerce Product Page** – Gallery > Details > Reviews > Related
- **Personal Brand** – Hero > Bio > Work > Testimonials > Contact
- **Blog / Editorial** – Header > Featured Post > Grid > Newsletter
