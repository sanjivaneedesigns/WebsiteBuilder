# Font Pairings

This document catalogs curated font pairing combinations for use across design energies and brand personalities.

## Overview

Typography sets the tone of a design. These pairings are selected for strong contrast between display and body typefaces.

## Pairing Categories

### Bold & Editorial
- Display: Playfair Display / Heading: Montserrat / Body: Inter

### Modern & Clean
- Display: Inter / Heading: Inter / Body: Inter

### Luxury & Refined
- Display: Cormorant Garamond / Heading: Raleway / Body: Lato

### Playful & Expressive
- Display: Syne / Heading: DM Sans / Body: DM Sans

### Tech & Geometric
- Display: Space Grotesk / Heading: Space Grotesk / Body: IBM Plex Sans
